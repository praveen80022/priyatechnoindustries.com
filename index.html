About Us

Priya Techno industries is an ISO 9001:2015 certified Company which was established in the year 2014 by Mr. Sushil Kumar Singh who is having more than 25 years of experience in machine shop and has a technical background [ B.Tech. in mechanical engineering ] . The company is based in Ludhiana, Punjab. Built in an area of 2000 sq. yard near Sahnewal Airport. It is a partnership firm.

We are involved in manufacturing of Automobiles steering knuckles and some other auto parts for domestic and export purpose. We are equipped with numerous types of machines and inspection instruments to produce world class quality products.
